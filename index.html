Ccv-object-tracking

User-Defined Blob Detection And Tracking in CCV.

In my application I propose to introduce Object/Pattern tracking into CCV in which the object/pattern to be tracked can be completely defined by the user.

The deliverables of this project will be:

  1. A tool for selection of templates, where user can select the particular template to match
  2. Template matching integrated into CCV.
  3. Making an MT application to get and send data over Bluetooth/ Wi-Fi network.
  4. Application for demonstration. Where you can transfer images from your MT screen to device just by moving it into the device region on the screen. This will be one use out of many, but will be the best for demonstration purpose.
